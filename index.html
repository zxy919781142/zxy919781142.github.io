Xinyi Zhao 赵心怡

Postdoctoral Researcher · Max Planck Institute for Human Development, Berlin

I am a computational social scientist working at the intersection of gender studies, science of science, and migration research. My work uses large-scale bibliometric, digital trace, and survey data to understand gender disparities in academic careers in an era of globalization and digitalization.
